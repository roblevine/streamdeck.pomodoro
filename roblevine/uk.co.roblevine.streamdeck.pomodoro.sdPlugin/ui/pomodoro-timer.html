Preview Preview Configure your Pomodoro cycle. Enter durations in mm:ss format (e.g., 25:00 for 25 minutes). Defaults: 00:10 work, 00:02 short break, 00:05 long break after 4 cycles. Short press = pause/resume. Long press (>2s) = reset to start. When "Pause At End Of Each Phase" is on, the next phase will wait for a press before starting; when off, phases auto-advance.
